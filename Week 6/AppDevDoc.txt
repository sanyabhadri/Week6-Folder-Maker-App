## 0. Genre

Genre:
Music

Explain why this genre fits your app idea:
Music is something people enjoy every day, but many users struggle to discover new artists that match their 
interests. This app focuses on helping users explore music based on their mood and favorite genres while giving 
independent artists more exposure.

---

## 1. Backstory

What happened before the app exists?
Many music streaming apps mainly recommend popular artists, making it difficult for users to discover smaller 
musicians. Independent artists also have a hard time reaching new audiences.

Who lives or works in this world?
Music listeners, independent artists, playlist creators, and music fans who enjoy discovering new songs.

What pressure, problem, confusion, or need exists?
Users feel overwhelmed by the huge number of songs available and often receive repetitive recommendations. 
Independent artists struggle to gain visibility without large marketing budgets.

---

## 2. Story / User Need

Who is the user?
A college student or music fan who enjoys listening to music every day and wants to find new artists.

What does the user need?
A simple way to discover music based on mood, favorite genres, and personal interests.

What problem are they trying to solve?
They are tired of hearing the same recommendations and want to discover new artists more easily.

Why does this matter to them?
Finding new music keeps listening exciting and helps users build playlists that match their personality and activities.

---

## 3. Product Definition

What are you building inside the story?
A music discovery and playlist-sharing app called BeatBridge.

What will the app help the user do?
- Discover new music based on mood.
- Save favorite songs.
- Create playlists.
- Follow independent artists.
- Share playlists with friends.

What is the smallest useful version of this app?

A basic app where users can create an account, choose favorite genres, receive music recommendations, 
and save songs into playlists.

## 5. Functions and Procedures

| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| CreateAccount | Creates a new user account | Username, email, password | New account created |
